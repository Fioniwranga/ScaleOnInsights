At Scale On Insight, we value the trust placed in us by our clients, partners, candidates, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information shared with us through our website and business operations.

Who We Are

Scale On Insight is a global e-commerce services agency that helps brands, sellers, and marketplaces improve their digital presence through product image editing, A+ content design, product listing optimization, content development, catalog management, and marketplace support services.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect information including:

Information You Provide

  • Full name & Contact info
  • Business Email Address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number
  • Company or brand name
  • Website URL
  • Project requirements & briefs
  • Service inquiries
  • Career application details
  • Resume, portfolio, and supporting documents

Information Collected Automatically

When you browse our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically:

  • IP address
  • Browser type & configurations
  • Device information & OS
  • Pages visited & scroll duration
  • Website performance & speed statistics

This details helps us improve website functionality, performance, and overall user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected may be processed and used for key purposes, including:

  • Responding to project inquiries, quotations, and consultation requests.
  • Evaluating client project scope and delivering agreed support services.
  • Communicating regarding project statuses, revisions, proposals, and updates.
  • Evaluating career applications, portfolios, and coordinate recruitment workflows.
  • Improving analytics, platform speed, and customer-facing interfaces.
  • Complying with necessary legal, regulatory, tax, or business accounting records.

Note: We do not sell, rent, license, or trade your personal information to any third parties.

Recruitment & Career Applications

Information submitted through our Careers page is used solely for recruitment, qualifications evaluation, and hiring processes. Resumes, portfolios, and applicant details are reviewed only by authorized administrative personnel involved in the hiring process, and are retained only for legitimate recruitment requirements.

Data Security

Scale On Insight implements reasonable and standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, misuse, loss, or disclosure. While we strive to protect your information, no online transmission or digital storage platform can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools for operations, including:

  • Website analytics & performance tracking
  • Secure database & Form submissions
  • Communication & customer emails
  • Cloud storage & Project management

These external providers operate under their own respective privacy, cookie, and security policies.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and understand visitor behavior. Users may disable cookies through browser settings; however, some website features or interactive modals may not function as intended. You can also decline non-essential cookies via our consent banner.

Intellectual Property & Client Data

Any materials, assets, product information, images, documents, or brand resources shared by clients remain the property of their respective owners. Scale On Insight treats all client-provided information as confidential and uses it solely for the execution of agreed services.

Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your personal information stored in our database.
  • Correction or update of inaccurate information.
  • Deletion of your personal details where legally permissible.
  • Withdrawal of consent for future communications and proposals.
